Derek's Frozen Broccoli

Description

Ingredients:

  • Frozen Broccoli
  • A microwave
  • A bowl
  • A scale

Steps:

  1. Grab your bowl and weigh out 6 ounces of broccoli
  2. Once it's weighed, throw it in the microwave for 4 minutes
  3. Once the 4 minutes is up then voila! Your broccoli is ready
