Derek's Chicken Breast

Description

Ingredients:

  • Six Chicken Breasts
  • Olive Oil
  • Keg's Chicken and Rib Spice
  • Garlic Powder
  • Chili Flakes
  • Optional: Low-Calorie Hot Sauce

Steps:

  1. Place each chicken breast in your air fryer.
  2. Then, use 1 tablespoon of olive oil and rub it into each chicken breast
  3. Next, liberally shake the Keg's chicken and rib spice all over each breast.
  4. Do the same thing for the garlic powder and chili flakes.
  5. Turn the air fryer to 390F and cook it for 17 minutes
  6. Flip it after 17 minutes and repeat steps 3 and 4.
  7. Turn the air fryer back on for 10 minutes until the internal temperature of the chicken is over 165F
  8. Take it out and enjoy
